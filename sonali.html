Meals on Wheels" is a program that delivers nutritious meals to individuals who are unable to prepare or access food on their own, often due to age, disability, or illness. The program typically serves seniors, people with disabilities, and individuals who are chronically or temporarily homebound. It aims to ensure that these individuals receive regular, balanced meals to support their health and well-being, as well as provide them with social interaction and a sense of connection to their community. The tagline reflects the program's commitment to providing nourishment, care, and support to those in need, wherever they may be.
